Strategies and Action Plans

The Kuwait National AI Strategy (2025-2028) outlines a strategic roadmap for integrating artificial intelligence into the country’s economic and social framework, aligning with Kuwait Vision 2035. The strategy aims to position the country as a leader in AI adoption by focusing on economic growth, workforce empowerment, and digital transformation while ensuring ethical and responsible AI deployment. Through collaboration, investment, and education, Kuwait seeks to create a knowledge-based economy and leverage AI for long-term national prosperity.

- Kuwait aims to become a leader in AI innovation by 2028, with AI deeply integrated into key sectors to enhance governance, economic growth, and public services.
- The strategy emphasises responsible AI deployment, prioritising privacy, security, and ethical considerations.
- The focus is on economic diversification, efficiency in public services, and workforce transformation to support AI-driven industries.
